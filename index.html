Curtis James Jackson III

Curtis "50 Cent" Jackson is an American rapper, actor, and entrepreneur who rose from the streets of Queens to become one of hip-hop's most successful artists. Known for his gritty lyrics and business acumen, 50 Cent transformed his street credibility into a multimedia empire. His debut album "Get Rich or Die Tryin'" became one of the best-selling rap albums of all time, while his business ventures span from music to television production, including the hit series "Power."
